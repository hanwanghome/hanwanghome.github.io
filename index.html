About

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Pengcheng Laboratory in Shenzhen, China. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Peking University advised by Prof. Hui Li.

My research focuses on high-speed data stream processing, including probabilistic data structures (sketch algorithms), hardware-software co-design (FPGA), and network measurement and telemetry. I am broadly interested in applying efficient streaming analytics to real-world systems in networking, distributed computing, and AI/ML applications including federated learning, graph neural networks, real-time AI inference, and privacy-preserving machine learning systems.

I am currently seeking postdoctoral or research scientist positions in the United States starting around July 2026. Feel free to reach out!
